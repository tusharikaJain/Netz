About Netz Infocom

Netz Infocom Pvt. Ltd. is a specialized optical fiber infrastructure company focused on OFC installation, maintenance and dark fiber leasing. Our teams include splicing experts, HDD operators and project managers experienced in large-scale deployments.

We partner with RailTel and multiple telecom operators to deliver reliable connectivity across urban and rural routes.

Mission

Deliver dependable fiber infrastructure through safety, skilled manpower and high-quality execution.

Vision

To be a trusted national partner for fiber deployment and network expansion.
